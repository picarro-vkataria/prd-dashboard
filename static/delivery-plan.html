Executive Summary

P-Cubed Pro (Project Horizon) is the cloud-native replacement for legacy P-Cubed — a next-generation SaaS platform for natural gas utilities covering boundary-based survey planning, field data collection, automated leak analysis, investigation workflows, and compliance reporting.

The V1 release targets October 1, 2026, delivering 28 of 41 PRD features across 8 milestones (MS-3 through V1 launch). Three milestones are already complete. The remaining 5 milestones run April 1 – September 30, 2026, covering ~26 weeks of execution with a team of X persons across engineering, GIS, QA, and cloud infrastructure.

V1 is scoped to deliver the core operational platform — onboarding, campaigns, driving, boundary management, and investigations — excluding OTA, Data Explorer, Emissions360, Safety360, and mobile/handheld features, which are targeted for V2.
